# Privacy Policy for RecipeHub

**Last Updated: November 17, 2025**

## Introduction

RecipeHub ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application RecipeHub (the "App").

**By using RecipeHub, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.**

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

When you use RecipeHub, we may collect:

- **Search queries**: Recipe searches and filters you apply
- **Saved recipes**: Recipes you bookmark or save for later
- **Preferences**: Dietary preferences, cuisine types, and app settings
- **User-generated content**: Notes, ratings, or comments you add to recipes

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect certain information when you use the App:

- **Usage data**: Features you use, recipes you view, time spent in the app
- **Device information**: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
- **Performance data**: App crashes, response times, and technical errors
- **API usage metrics**: How often you access recipe data (tracked for service optimization)
- **Cache data**: Locally stored recipe information for offline access

### 1.3 Information from Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

- **Firebase Analytics**: Collects anonymous usage statistics and app performance data
- **Firebase Crashlytics**: Collects crash reports and diagnostic information
- **Spoonacular API**: Recipe data is retrieved from Spoonacular's database
- **Google Play Services**: For app distribution and updates

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

### 2.1 Core Functionality
- Provide recipe search and discovery features
- Save and sync your favorite recipes
- Personalize recipe recommendations based on your preferences
- Enable offline access through cached data

### 2.2 Service Improvement
- Analyze usage patterns to improve the app
- Monitor and fix technical issues and crashes
- Optimize API usage and app performance
- Develop new features based on user behavior

### 2.3 Communication
- Respond to your support requests and feedback
- Send important updates about the app (if you opt-in)
- Notify you of new features or changes to our policies

### 2.4 Legal Compliance
- Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service
- Protect against fraud, abuse, or illegal activity

## 3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 3.1 We DO NOT Sell Your Personal Information

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

### 3.2 Third-Party Service Providers

We share limited information with trusted service providers:

| Service | Purpose | Data Shared | Privacy Policy |
|---------|---------|-------------|----------------|
| **Firebase (Google)** | Analytics, crash reporting, cloud storage | Usage data, device info, anonymous identifiers | [Firebase Privacy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy) |

### 3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, such as:
- To comply with a subpoena or legal process
- To protect our rights, property, or safety
- To investigate fraud or security issues

## 4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

- **Encryption**: All data transmitted between the app and servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
- **Secure storage**: Local data is stored securely on your device
- **Firebase Security Rules**: Cloud data is protected with access controls
- **API key protection**: API keys are secured and rotated regularly
- **Regular security audits**: We monitor for vulnerabilities and threats

**However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.** While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

---

## 5. Data Retention

- **Saved recipes and preferences**: Stored as long as you use the app; deleted when you clear app data or uninstall
- **Usage analytics**: Retained for up to 14 months for analysis purposes
- **Crash logs**: Retained for 90 days for debugging
- **Cache data**: Automatically cleared after 7 days or when storage is full

---

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

### 6.1 Access and Update
- View your saved recipes and preferences in the app settings
- Modify or delete your preferences at any time

### 6.2 Data Deletion
- Clear all saved recipes and cache data through app settings
- Uninstall the app to remove all local data
- Contact us to request deletion of data stored on our servers

### 6.3 Opt-Out Options
- **Disable analytics**: You can opt-out of Firebase Analytics in app settings
- **Revoke permissions**: Manage app permissions in your device settings
- **Limit data collection**: Use the app offline to minimize data collection

### 6.4 Do Not Track
Currently, we do not respond to "Do Not Track" signals as there is no industry standard for compliance.

## 7. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at **privacy@recipehub.com**, and we will delete such information.
